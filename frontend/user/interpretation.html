Temperature

Criteria

drinking water -> maximum of 15°C for aesthetics
aquatic life -> ±1°C, allowable change from natural level
aquatic life (salmonids)
   18-19°C maximum weekly average for adults and juveniles
   8-10°C maximum weekly average for spawning
   13-15°C maximum weekly average for embryo survival
recreation -> 15-35°C, range for bathing

pH Level

Criteria

drinking water -> 6.5-8.5
aquatic life -> generally 6.5-9.0 unless background levels are otherwise and unique fauna and flora exist (i.e., boggy areas with pH below 6.5, marl lakes with pH above 9.0)
livestock watering -> 5.0-9.5
irrigation -> 5.0-9.0
recreation -> 5.0-9.0

Turbidity

Criteria

drinking water at the point of consumption
   -> 1 NTU maximum (health),
   -> 5 NTU maximum (aesthetics)
aquatic life
   -> 5 NTU increase when background = 50 NTU
   -> 10% increase when background > 50 NTU
